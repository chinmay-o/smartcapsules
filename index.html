          Upcoming Projects

          Our core idea is to provide a traveling experience you have never encountered before. We have an upcoming lineup of innovative products that we can’t wait to share with you anymore.

          Container Villa

          The container villas are built with a magnificent usage of space. They are designed to have a comfortable living area, a kitchen, and a bathroom. The container villas also have a spacious balcony where you can enjoy a beautiful view of the surroundings.

          Camper Van

          The all-new camper van is packed with amazing features that will make your camping experience more enjoyable than ever. It has a spacious interior with plenty of room for all your gear, and a comfortable bed for a good night's sleep.

          Vanity Van

          Introducing the vanity van, your own personal retreat that comes complete with all the amenities of home. From a cozy bed to a fully stocked kitchen, this van has everything you need to relax and rejuvenate. Best of all, it can go anywhere you do, so you can always have your home away from home with you.
